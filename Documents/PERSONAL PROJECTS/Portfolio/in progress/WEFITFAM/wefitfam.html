We build meaningful communities with free quality fitness

It doesn’t matter who
you are.

Access to quality fitness is a fundamental right and should be available to everyone.

FitFam is a social, volunteer-led fitness community that provides free and consistent access to quality fitness, wellness, and an overall active lifestyle year round. Our journey started with only six friends who were looking to stay active during the long and cold winter in Shanghai in 2015.

Through word of mouth and genuine appreciation for one another, our community has grown to over 100,000 members across 19 cities worldwide; a community that is built on trust, accountability, and inclusivity.

We’re your motivation boost to build a healthy lifestyle.
